FLATSIFY

UI, Branding

The platform was a service design project while working as a designer in PlastiqHaus Ltd, Edinburgh UK. Flatsify is a platform for the real estate market. Flatsify provides all the functionalities of a booking service, plus it uses social media and location data to provide additional services for people that seek to rent property. Following the AIRBNB logic of providing experiences rather than just a room to rent, Flatsify is developing a similar strategy targeting to longer term renting - professional trips, student accommodation and/or short let at a 6-12 month rental timeline. It seeks to provide a platform for flat rental but also services for the clients such as socializing, dating, entertainment, trips and other experiences.

I contributed to product research, social data research, UI design and branding in various stages of the project.
